  • What services does Ravonics offer?
    We deliver six core capabilities: Advanced Artificial Intelligence (neural networks, decision systems), Autonomous & Robotic Systems (unmanned platforms, swarm coordination), Intelligence & Decision Systems (multi-domain fusion, predictive analytics), Secure Communications & Cryptography (post-quantum encryption, resilient networks), High-Performance Computing (edge computing, distributed systems), and Systems Engineering & Integration (JADC2-compatible architecture, enterprise solutions).
    How do I engage Ravonics for a project?
    Reach out through our secure contact form or call our Ravenswood headquarters. We'll schedule a discovery session (classified or unclassified), assess technical feasibility, and develop a tailored solution architecture. For active RFPs, we respond within 48 hours.
    Do you work with commercial clients?
    Our primary focus is federal and defense consulting, though we selectively engage with commercial partners on strategic technology initiatives.
    What technologies does Ravonics specialize in?
    Led by an award-winning emerging tech director from Silicon Valley, our team specializes in AI platforms, autonomous systems, quantum-resistant cryptography, intelligence fusion, and systems integration. We build reusable platforms—not one-off custom solutions.
    What is the typical project timeline?
    We deliver lab-to-battlefield results in months, not years. Rapid prototypes deploy in 3-6 months, while platform development typically ranges from 6-12 months. Our agile methodology accelerates timelines without sacrificing quality or security.
  • What security clearances does Ravonics maintain?
    Our personnel hold TS/SCI clearances as required. We maintain CMMC Level 3 certification, NIST 800-171 compliance, and operate SCIF-capable facilities in Ravenswood, WV—ready for classified work from day one.
    How does Ravonics protect sensitive information?
    We implement multi-layered security protocols including encrypted communications, secure development environments, and strict access controls aligned with federal standards.
    Are your facilities SCIF-capable?
    Yes, we maintain secure facilities suitable for classified work and can coordinate with agency SCIFs as required for specific projects.
    Do you comply with ITAR and EAR regulations?
    Absolutely. We maintain strict compliance with International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) and Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
    What cybersecurity standards do you follow?
    We adhere to NIST 800-53, NIST 800-171, and DoD cybersecurity requirements, with continuous monitoring and regular security audits.
  • What contract vehicles does Ravonics support?
    We work through various federal contract vehicles including GSA Schedules, IDIQs, and direct contracts with agencies.
    Are you a registered government contractor?
    Yes, Ravonics is registered in SAM.gov and maintains active certifications required for federal contracting.
    Do you have small business certifications?
    Please contact us directly to discuss our current certifications and socioeconomic qualifications for set-aside opportunities.
    How do I request a proposal or quote?
    Submit RFPs or RFQs through our secure portal or contact our business development team directly for proposal submissions.
    What is your past performance record?
    Perfect past performance ratings. Zero failed audits. Projects delivered on time and under budget for DoD, IC, and federal agencies. Our work has briefed Congress and deployed to combatant commands. References available upon request.
