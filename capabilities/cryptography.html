Post-Quantum Cryptography & Blockchain for Defense Systems

Ravonics delivers next-generation cryptographic systems and distributed ledger technologies engineered for classified federal missions. Our post-quantum encryption protects against emerging quantum threats while blockchain provides tamper-proof audit trails and distributed trust for supply chain integrity. From hardware security modules to zero-knowledge proofs, we secure mission-critical data with mathematically provable protections that withstand sophisticated adversary attacks.

Military-grade encryption that maintains security even when quantum computing threatens traditional cryptography.

Key Capabilities

Post-Quantum Cryptography

NIST-compliant algorithms including lattice-based and hash-based cryptography protect against future quantum computing attacks, ensuring classified data remains secure as quantum capabilities mature.

Blockchain & Distributed Ledger

Tamper-proof audit trails and smart contracts for supply chain integrity, ammunition tracking, and multi-party workflows with cryptographically verified provenance and immutable records.

Hardware Security Modules (HSMs)

FIPS 140-3 Level 4 validated tamper-resistant cryptographic processors protect key material with physical safeguards meeting NSA High Assurance Platform requirements for classified systems.

Zero-Knowledge Proofs

Cryptographic protocols enabling verification without revealing underlying data—critical for cross-domain information sharing and privacy-preserving authentication in coalition environments.

Cryptographic Key Management

Enterprise key lifecycle management with automated rotation, secure distribution, and emergency revocation across distributed systems ensuring continuous cryptographic operations at scale.

Side-Channel Attack Prevention

Countermeasures against power analysis, timing attacks, and electromagnetic emanations protecting cryptographic implementations from sophisticated hardware exploitation and reverse engineering.
